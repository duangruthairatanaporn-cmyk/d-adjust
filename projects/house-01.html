House / 01

House 01

A calm residential composition shaped by shade, breathing space, and a warm material rhythm tuned to tropical light.

ResidenceThailand2026

Concept

Quiet geometry, sheltered thresholds, and soft daylight.

The project is composed as a sequence of pauses: arrival, threshold, shaded living, and courtyard light.

Spatial idea

The plan is organized around a protected interior void, allowing daylight and cross-ventilation to move gently through the house.

Material tone

Warm concrete, timber, and muted mineral surfaces build a calm envelope that deepens at dusk.

Atmosphere

The house is designed to feel grounded and quiet, with transitions that slow the pace through the plan.
